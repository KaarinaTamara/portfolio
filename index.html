Welcome! My name is Kaarina Nieminen and I want to thank you for checking out my web dev portfolio, I hope this shows you how capable I am to fulfill all your web development needs.

I am a recent graduate of Juno College's Immersive Bootcamp. Through this opportunity, I have been able to create a variety of responsive and accessible sites both as solo assignments and in collaboration with others. Check out my projects section below to see some of the exciting work I've been involved with so far!

Having spent the last 11 years in telecommunication sales I am beyond excited to head straight into this exciting career change in the world of web development. I am passionate about providing fun, interactive and accessible websites and to find my place in a world where I can celebrate diversity and inclusion. I work best with a team and strive for conflict resolution no matter the circumstances.

Make sure you return here to see more work in the future. I, and this portfolio, are constantly growing!

Katsella

HTML | CSS | React | Firebase
Katsella is a social media based application to share movie and tv show recommendations amongst your friends. The name Katsella is a Finnish word for "watch", this combines both my Finnish heritage and Juno College's love for Godzilla. And this is why we see the cat-Godzilla hybrid mascot on the home page. Using Google's Firebase to store and update the users inputs in real time this app allows you to suggest shows to friends and leave comments and ratings for anyone who has access to your personalized list.

Timeless

HTML | CSS | JavaScript | RESTful API
Timeless was built with a development team of four creaters. The app is the ultimate meeting planner for any professional. It allows you to select meeting times for various attendees, including those in different timezones. When working with those in varying timezones the app also takes the time changes into account and ensures a time is selected that falls between 8am to 7pm in the attendees own area. Once a meeting is booked, all attendees can view, edit and book any other necessary meetings.
